Project brief

Intro: As a leader in the outdoor clothing and gear market, as well as a champion of environmental stewardship, REI wants to make it easier for people to enjoy the great outdoors.

Mission: Provide families and individuals with a tool to help them plan for outdoor activities like camping with limited time and budget.

WHO is the user? Campers and outdoors enthusiasts.

WHAT is the user problem / need / experience? User have problem to plan a trip, find activities they want at a camping ground, find new camping places, affordability and time management.

WHEN does it occur? around summer time or whenever users plan for camping.

WHERE does it occur? at home or camps

WHY does it occur :
1. lack of communication
2. lack of information
3. short on time
4. limited budget
5. Inefficient event management

HOW will a design help solve it? eliminate all obstacles to enable the users to go on a camping trip more often

WHO is the user? Campers and outdoors enthusiasts.

WHAT will the interaction between the user and the design be? Users will use the product to find camp sites through search and discovery system, Plan a trip alone or with a group through a checklist like system and buy/rent any equipment needed for that trip through REI.

WHEN will the user be interacting with the design? mostly during planing phase to any trip but user can use it on site if needed to find activities / products onsite.

WHERE does the interaction occur? any where even on camp site.

WHY does it occur:
1. Users need all information checked and in place to eliminate errors
2. Users want to manage the time and cost
3. Users sometimes don't know what gear they need
4. Users sometimes cant afford buying everything by themselves
5. Users don't where they want to go

HOW will the design help the user? Save time and money for the user and have a better cooperative experience.
